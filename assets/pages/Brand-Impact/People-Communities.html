SOCIAL PURPOSE


We’re in the chicken business, but we’re also in the people business.


With nearly one million team members serving thousands of communities, we have a passion for fighting inequality by feeding people’s potential.


Our global food donation program shares surplus protein-rich chicken to those who need it while our upskilling programs for youth, women, refugees and people with disabilities creates confidence and unlocks opportunities for a brighter future. And we work alongside incredible nonprofit partners to do it.

Equity, Inclusion & Belonging


We foster an inclusive culture where everyone is welcomed and valued. Every day, we work with active allyship at our core and ensure our teams reflect the communities we serve.


In 2024, we announced that we have achieved gender balance across our global leadership team and restaurant support centers.


We proudly gather two Communities of Belonging which every employee across the globe is welcome to join…


SHINE is dedicated to strengthening representation, empowering women at every level of leadership and providing transformative development opportunities and experiences in leadership and transformative development opportunities and experiences.


Kentucky Fried Pride is dedicated to supporting and celebrating the LGBTQ+ community and its allies, thereby creating a true sense of belonging for all.
